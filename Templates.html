Skwela

I looked at 4 different templates and ended up going with this one thats more education oriented. The other templates were targeted for blogs, portfolios, and education which were good choices as well, but this one had some design I preferred. It's multi page and has a modern look thats also appropriate for teaching content which is something that will fit this course material. The other templates also had some more code files I didn't recognize, but really they all have an amount of files that's more than plenty to absorb. Now It's just a matter of analyzing the code in this template and checking what code I won't need at all and what will be useful. I'd hate to accidentally delete code that's important to the integrity of the overall site so I will have to carefully interact with it. I'm also glad we had an extra day to submit this work.

About
